VANISHING ROOTS: THE SACRIFICE OF DAYAKS

BY GREEN WELFARE INDONESIA

" "Vanishing Roots" delicately intertwines culture and environment. It portrays the poignant saga of the Dayak tribe in Kalimantan, Indonesia, whose cherished forests, the embodiment of their cultural heritage, have been relinquished to capitalism, primarily for palm oil plantations since the 1970s. This evocative artwork deftly contrasts the vibrant, culturally rich ancestral lands of Dayak with the stark, lifeless aftermath of destruction. This piece ignites a profound conversation about the urgency of environmental preservation and safeguarding of Indonesian indigenous cultures."


Designed & Illustrated by Kenneth Immanuel Tobing Curated by Nala Amirah 2023, Green Welfare Indonesia
