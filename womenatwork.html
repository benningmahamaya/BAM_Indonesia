WOMEN AT WORK

BY PRINCESS AINSLEY LESILOLO

"Above all else, family stands as the cherished cornerstone of Indonesian society. Across the generational tapestry of women in this diverse archipelago, an unwavering commitment unfolds as they tirelessly pour their efforts into safeguarding the well-being of those they hold dear. The ceaseless march of time merely serves to illuminate the enduring dedication of Indonesian women in their noble endeavour to provide for their beloved family."
