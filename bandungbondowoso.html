BANDUNG BONDOWOSO

BY WINSTON MULIAWAN

"Bandung Bondowoso, The Sorceress Knight Legend of the Creation of Candi Prambanan, the largest Hindu temple in Indonesia. It is said that a powerful knight named Bandung Bondowoso was the one who built the temple. He was first deployed to attack the Prambanan kingdom. After Bandung Bondowoso killed the king, he saw Roro Jonggrang, the princess of the Prambanan Kingdom. It was love at first sight for Bandung Bondowoso, but not so for Roro Jonggrang. He would only accept it if he was able to build 1000 Candi in one night. To win her heart, Bandung Bondowoso summoned his terrifying army of genies and used all of his mighty Sorcery."
