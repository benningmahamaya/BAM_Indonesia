OUR EXHIBITION LOCATION

Beppu Art Month takes place in the charming city of Beppu, located in Oita Prefecture, Japan. Renowned for its natural hot springs and stunning mountainous landscapes, Beppu provides a picturesque backdrop for this cultural celebration. The event venues are scattered throughout the city, offering visitors the opportunity to explore Beppu's unique blend of traditional and contemporary architecture while enjoying a diverse range of art exhibitions, performances, and installations. Whether you're strolling through the historic streets or soaking in one of Beppu's famous hot springs, the city's beauty and charm are sure to leave a lasting impression.
