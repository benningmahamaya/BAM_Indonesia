TUGU MUDA

BY TAN OLIVER CHRISTIAN SUTANTIO

"Over sixty years of age, Tugu Muda (Indonesian "Youth Monument") is a definite icon of the city of Semarang, Central Java, my hometown. Erected in commemoration of the struggle of the Indonesian youth for the sake of independence, especially in the Five Day Battle against Imperial Japan in 1945; many young men shed their lifeblood on the streets of Semarang, but not without a cause, for they know that freedom requires a price worth paying for. This monument is special to me and stands as a reminder that when you believe in something, you have to fight for it."
