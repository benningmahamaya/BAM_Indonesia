BEPPU ART MONTH

Known as one of the world's leading hot spring resorts, Beppu City in Oita Prefecture has long been a thriving tourist destination. In recent years, the town has seen a flourishing of cultural activities organized by its citizens, including art exhibitions, dance performances, music events, and film screenings. "Beppu Art Month" is a citizen cultural festival held in Beppu City, organized by the Mixed Bathing World Executive Committee, which brings together a variety of events related to culture and the arts.

This initiative aims to promote citizen participation and the development of small-scale cultural organizations by providing support for event planning, publicity, and secretariat services, thereby fostering a vibrant cultural and artistic community in Beppu City.
