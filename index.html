WHAT IS

BEPPU ART MONTH?

In the charming Japanese city of Beppu, known for its hot springs and vibrant art scene, Beppu Art Month, an annual celebration of art and culture, is held. Showcasing the work of both local and international artists, this month-long event takes place in October and offers a varied array of art exhibitions, installations, performances, and workshops.

Beppu Art Month invites tourists to discover the city's rich artistic tradition while taking in the beauty of its natural surroundings. Its goals are to foster artistic expression, cultural exchange, and community engagement. Everyone can enjoy Beppu Art Month, regardless of their level of interest in art—you could be a casual observer, an art enthusiast, or someone just seeking out a distinctive cultural experience.

BEPPU ART MONTH'S

INDONESIA EXHIBITION

Beppu Art Month's Indonesia Exhibition is a captivating showcase of Indonesian art and culture, curated as part of the larger cultural festival in Beppu, Japan.

This exhibition highlights the rich artistic heritage of Indonesia, featuring a diverse range of artworks from Indonesian artists. Through this exhibition, visitors have the opportunity to immerse themselves in the vibrant colors, intricate designs, and compelling narratives that define Indonesian art, fostering cultural exchange and appreciation between Indonesia and Japan.

OUR LATEST EXHIBITION

KUNO '国の'

Showcasing the sight and sounds of Indonesia, our latest Beppu Art Month's Indonesia Exhibition, KUNO, took place from November 10th to 12th, 2023. The exhibition was held at Bluebird Kitahama ブルーバード in Beppu-shi, Kitahama, with performances scheduled from 12:00 to 20:00 (11th November till 21:00).

The captivating beauty of Indonesian music, dance, and fashion in the exhibition, showcased the rich cultural heritage of Indonesia.
