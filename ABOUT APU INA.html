ASIA PACIFIC UNIVERSITY INDONESIA STUDENT BODY

A vital component of the Beppu Art Month committee, the Indonesian student body at Asia Pacific University (APU) in Beppu adds to the event's vibrant cultural scene.

The Indonesian students at APU contribute their own viewpoint and imaginative flair to the committee, enhancing the festival with their artistic abilities and cultural background. Their participation demonstrates the open and varied aspects of Beppu Art Month, as well as the event's international scope and collaborative spirit.
